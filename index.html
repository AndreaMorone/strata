A discreet MVC interface for Wordpress

Why?

Wordpress is a fantastic bloging engine and is more and more used as a full fledged CMS. When building more complex websites or applications, one may find that you can't quite cleanly separate the business logic from the templating engine.

Wordpress MVC allows you to link permalinks with Controller actions to offer you an elegant coding method and keep the templates as clean as possible along with helping with customized database entries and view helpers.

How?

Wordpress MVC is a Composer dependency that can be added to your application.

Once the MVC is downloaded and configured, it stays out of the way until you need it.
